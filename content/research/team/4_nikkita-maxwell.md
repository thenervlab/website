bio: "Nikkita recently completed her honours project focused on the climate resilience of Fijian Lasioglossum bees. Her research employed proteomic approaches to identify key drivers of heat tolerance in these important pollinators. She successfully annotated proteins relevant to thermotolerance from the genome of these bees and characterised the molecular mechanisms of thermal stability across specialist species found at different elevations. Her findings bridge the gap between physiological heat resilience and the thermal stability of the bee proteome, contributing to a deeper understanding of how these species adapt to climate change. Nikkita will shortly commence her PhD candidature, focusing on understanding the cellular impact of HERV-targetting therapeutics."
